DRAWING



I also like drawing in my freetime. It is one of my favourite hobbies which I've been doing since a long time

I started learning how to draw from a young age from watching shows and trying to copy artstyles. As time passes I developed my own skills and styles while also trying new methods of drawing and creating artworks

Nowadays I mainly focus on digital drawings, where I would make fanarts or studies from shows or artstyles that I like. After being more confident with my skills I also started doing comissions for people through websites such as Fiverr, Twitter and Instagram

Besides that I also delve in drawing comics, mangas and webtoons. I like using artworks as means to express my creativity and share my stories

The comics and webtoons I made are mostly for competitions or comissions for my online clients
